Nagora Store Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Nagora Store is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we may collect, how it is used, and the measures taken to safeguard your data. By using Nagora Store, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information Collected

2.1 Personal Information

Nagora Store does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app is designed solely for demonstration purposes and does not engage in user registration, authentication, or any form of data collection.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system, and app usage statistics. This data is used for analytical purposes to improve the app's performance and user experience.

3. Use of Information

3.1 Analytical Purposes

Non-personal information collected is used for analytics to understand user behavior, identify trends, and enhance Nagora Store's functionality.

3.2 Communication

If users choose to contact us via the provided email address (contact@nagora.store), the information shared in the communication will be used solely for the purpose of responding to inquiries, collaboration requests, or feedback.

4. Data Security

Nagora Store employs industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we strive to implement robust safeguards.

5. Third-Party Services

Nagora Store does not integrate with third-party services that may collect user data. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any external links or services accessed through the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Nagora Store is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Nagora Store may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal or operational requirements. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@nagora.store.
